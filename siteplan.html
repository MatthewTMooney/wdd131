Cards: The Battle – Site Plan

A strategic deck-building card game using a standard 52-card deck, where each card has a unique effect.

Site Name

Playing Cards: The Battle

You are clashing with Cards. It's generic, and is supposed to be reminiscent of trading card games like Yu-Gi-Oh or Magic: The Gathering, and is so the site is named after the game

Optional Domain: cardsthebattle.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Cards: The Battle website is to provide a central hub where players can:

  • Learn the game rules and mechanics
  • Browse all card effects and strategies
  • Build and save their own custom 10-card decks

The site will focus on both educating new players and supporting advanced strategy for competitive play.

Scenarios

  • “How can I view all the card effects for each suit and rank?”
  • “Can I build a custom deck and save it for later?”
  • “Where can I find the full rules to teach my friends quickly?”

Color Schema

  • Deep Red (#B22222): Used for headings, accents, and buttons, similar to Hearts and Diamonds on playing cards.
  • Ivory White (#FFFFF0): Used for the background to emulate standard playing cards
  • Dark Gray (#333333): Used for body text to ensure readability.
